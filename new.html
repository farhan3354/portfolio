Pavlović Nursery

The Pavlović Nursery is a family-run company that produces decorative seedlings for landscaping parks and gardens. We produce seedlings of conifers, spruces, ornamental shrubs, ferns and seedlings needed for hedges. In order to round out our offer, we have started the production of grass turf - grass carpet.

We offer our clients a complete service, professional advice on choosing plants, creating a conceptual design for landscaping, transporting plants, carrying out landscaping work, preparing the terrain, planting plants and advice on maintenance.

We invite you to visit the nursery and see for yourself the range and quality of our products.
